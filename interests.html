" this is what I look like when I'm laughing ... "

Before college, I had never seen myself laugh. My first year at MIT someone took a candid photo of me laughing. I was shocked - I looked so different. Since then I've been obsessed with showing people what they look like when they're completely off guard, comfortable, and happy. So many people fear getting their photos taken, what they don't know is that everyone is photogenic when they're laughing, when they're happy and carefree.

That's always my aim: to capture these beautiful moments.
